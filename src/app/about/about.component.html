About Young Chef

Hello and welcome to Young Chef!

We all need to eat and it's a vital life skill to be able to cook for oneself.

The intention of the Young Chef application is to encourage and teach kids of all ages to learn how to cook. The user friendly design makes it easy to navigate so you can get to cooking quickly without any prior kitchen or cooking knowledge. Although Young Chef started as a final project for the Front-End Bootcamp at Grand Circus, our intention is to maintain and grow the app moving forward.

If you’d like to partner with Young Chef for future educational purposes reach out to one of our team members.

Tech used: Angular, TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, API
