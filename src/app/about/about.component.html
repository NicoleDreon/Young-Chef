Hello and welcome to Young Chef!

We all need to eat and it's a vital life skill to be able to cook for oneself.

Young Chef started as a final project for Frontend Bootcmap at Grand Circus. The intention of the application is to encourage and teach kids of all ages to learn how to cook. The user friendly design makes it easy to navigate so you can get to cooking quickly without any prior kitchen or cooking knowledge.
